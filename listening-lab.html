Listening and Pronunciation Lab

Practise cruise announcements, service dialogues, safety instructions, directions, and operational reporting.

Unit 1: Ship Orientation

Transcript

Welcome aboard MV Ocean Horizon. Guest services is located on Deck 4 beside the main atrium. The medical center is on Deck 2, and the shore excursions desk is on Deck 5. Crew members must follow the chain of command and report safety concerns immediately.

Listening task: Write the location, time, requested action, and one professional phrase you hear.

Unit 2: Embarkation & Reception

Transcript

Good afternoon and welcome aboard. May I see your cruise card, please? Thank you. Your stateroom is 8246 on Deck 8, forward. Take the elevator to Deck 8, turn left, and follow the signs. Your safety drill begins at 1600.

Listening task: Write the location, time, requested action, and one professional phrase you hear.

Unit 3: Cabin & Housekeeping

Transcript

Housekeeping, good morning. May I come in? Thank you. I have brought the extra towels. I also understand that the air-conditioning is not cooling. I will contact maintenance immediately and call you again within twenty minutes.

Listening task: Write the location, time, requested action, and one professional phrase you hear.

Unit 4: Food & Beverage

Transcript

Good evening. Before I take your order, may I ask whether you have any food allergies? Thank you for telling me about your nut allergy. I will inform the galley and confirm which dishes can be prepared safely.

Listening task: Write the location, time, requested action, and one professional phrase you hear.

Unit 5: Guest Relations

Transcript

I am sorry that the noise disturbed your sleep. I understand why you are upset. I will contact the duty manager, review the cabin location, and check the beverage charge. I will update you within fifteen minutes.

Listening task: Write the location, time, requested action, and one professional phrase you hear.

Unit 6: Safety & Emergencies

Transcript

Attention, please. Smoke has been reported on Deck 7 aft. Guests on Deck 7 must proceed to the forward stairway and follow crew instructions. Do not use the elevators. Guests requiring assistance should remain with a crew member.

Listening task: Write the location, time, requested action, and one professional phrase you hear.

Unit 7: Ports & Excursions

Transcript

Good morning. Tour group Blue will meet in the main theater at 0815. Please bring your cruise card and tour ticket. The excursion departs at 0830. Ship time and local time are the same today. All guests must return by 1700.

Listening task: Write the location, time, requested action, and one professional phrase you hear.
