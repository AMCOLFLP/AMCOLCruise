Common Cruise Ship English Errors

Replace weak, direct, or unclear expressions with professional cruise-service language.

Weak ExpressionImproved Professional Expression
You wait here.Please wait here while I confirm the information.
Not my department.Let me contact the correct department for you.
Your room has problem.A maintenance issue has been reported in your stateroom.
What food cannot eat?Do you have any food allergies or dietary requirements?
Calm down.I understand that this situation is frustrating.
Go to emergency place.Please proceed to your assigned muster station.
Bus come late.The return bus is expected to arrive fifteen minutes late.
I promise refund.I will refer your request to the duty manager for authorization.

Thai Assist

Bilingual example: Please proceed to your muster station. — กรุณาไปยังสถานีรวมพลของท่าน
