Pre-Test and Post-Test

The post-test unlocks after all seven units and the capstone are completed.

Diagnostic Pre-Test

1. Which phrase is best when welcoming a guest?
2. Where is navigation controlled?
3. Which direction means toward the back of the ship?
4. Which phrase correctly checks an allergy?
5. What is a muster station?
6. Which is the most professional complaint response?
7. What should be included in directions?
8. Which sentence reports completed work?
9. What does all-aboard time mean?
10. Which communication is safety-critical?

Performance Tasks

  2. Write a short safety announcement.
