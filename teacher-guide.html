Teacher Guide, Answer Keys, and Rubrics

Support for integrated reading, listening, speaking, writing, service, and safety assessment.

Suggested Weighting

ComponentWeight
Unit checks30%
Listening and speaking25%
Reading and writing20%
Capstone simulation25%

Performance Criteria

Assess clarity, accuracy, politeness, vocabulary, grammar, pronunciation, listening response, service recovery, safety language, and teamwork.

Teaching Method

  1. Preview the visual and vocabulary.
  2. Model the dialogue or announcement.
  3. Practise controlled language.
  4. Run the case study.
  5. Complete the reflection and unit check.

Thai Assist

Bilingual example: Please proceed to your muster station. — กรุณาไปยังสถานีรวมพลของท่าน
